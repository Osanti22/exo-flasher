Before you start

  • Use the right browser. Chrome Edge on desktop, or Chrome 148+ on Android with a USB-C OTG cable. Safari iPhone/iPad Firefox will not work.
  • Use a DATA USB cable, not a charge-only one. If the unit never appears in the port picker, the cable is the usual cause.
  • Click Connect, pick the serial port in the pop-up, then flash. The image is about 9.6 MB and takes roughly a minute.

Logs & console

Open a serial port to watch a board's output. Use a different port to flash and log at the same time, or pick the flashing port and it switches over automatically. It streams until you click Close. Flashing output also shows here.
