Before you start

  • Use the right browser. Chrome Edge on desktop, or Chrome 148+ on Android with a USB-C OTG cable. Safari iPhone/iPad Firefox will not work.
  • Use a DATA USB cable, not a charge-only one. If the unit never appears in the port picker, the cable is the usual cause.
  • Click Connect, pick the serial port in the pop-up, then flash. The image is about 9.6 MB and takes roughly a minute.

How to confirm it worked

After flashing, click Open serial monitor and watch the boot log (or the Logs tab). You succeeded if you see either of these lines: 

IMU LH up (V3.1 single: cs=17, rst/wake0 via PCA9534@0x20, no-INT, accel+gyro+rotvec)
IMU LH up (Version#1 single: cs=17, rst=8 wake=18 direct-GPIO, no-INT, accel+gyro+rotvec)

You only ever see one. The same image works on both board revisions - the firmware auto-detects V3.1 or Version #1 and picks the right IMU driver.

Troubleshooting

  • Port doesn't appear? Try a different data cable and USB port.
  • Unit disconnects and reconnects on its own? A blank board can briefly drop off the USB bus. Just click Connect again.
  • A badly-flashed unit won't connect? Hold BOOT while plugging in (keep it held until the port appears), then release and Connect.
